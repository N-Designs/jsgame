How to play Memory Card Game?

Remark 1

Pick 2 cards having the same content

Remark 2

Get the 8 matched in a less moves

Remark 3

There is a time for playing this game. when you pass all matched cards the game end

Remark 4

An alert message will be appeared to evaluate your memory skills and give you stars

Remark 5

Your score will be displayed under the game in a table

Remark 6

The table will display the highscore of the player
